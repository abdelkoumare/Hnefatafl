Brandubh Rules

Win Conditions (Corner Escape)

  • Attackers win by capturing the King.
  • Defenders win by moving the King onto any of the corner squares on the board.

Movement

  • All pieces move like rooks in chess - they can move any number of squares along either the row or column they are in.
  • A piece cannot move through another piece.
  • Only the king can be on the central throne square, however other pieces can move through it.
  • Only the king can move onto the corner squares.

Capturing Pieces

  • All pieces except for the King are captured if they are pinched between two opposing pieces - horizontally or vertically.
  • A piece can also be captured if it is pinched between an opposing piece and an empty throne square.
  • A piece can also be captured if it is pinched between an opposing piece and one of the corner squares.
  • The capture only counts if a move of the opponent created the pinch. Thus, it is safe for a piece to move between two opposing pieces on it's turn.
  • It is possible to capture multiple pieces in the same turn.

Capturing the King

  • If the King is on the central square, he must be surrounded on all four sides by attackers in order to be captured.
  • If the King is adjacent to the central square, the King is captured if he is surrounded on three sides by attackers, and on the fourth side by the center square.
  • If the King is not on the central square or adjacent to the central square, then he is captured like a regular piece.
  • Note that the King can be captured if he is between an opposing piece and a corner square.

Additional Conditions

  • If a player cannot move, then he loses the game.
